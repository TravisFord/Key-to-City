Brass Keys

We have made Keys for over 600 Cities and Towns across America!

ALL of our keys come giftboxed.

Our Keys come in three colors, Antique Silver, Antique Gold, and Antique Bronzed. They are very Beautiful in its own right, but possess an ageing antique and modern and professional look. Some colors go better with your logo than others. We will be happy to offer our suggestions to you, on what would look the best. Each key is flat on the back, making it ideal to mount on a plaque, with an inscription for the receiver. Each key is gift boxed, and also has an eyelet in the handle, for placing a lanyard so you can hang the key around someone’s neck.
