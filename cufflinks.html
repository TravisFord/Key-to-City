Custom Made Cufflinks

Our Cufflinks can be done in Hard Enamel, (Cloisonné), die struck, etc. Many people have them made exactly like their lapel pins. The keys come in a gift box. You can have them placed in a deluxe felt gift box if you wish. You can order as few as 150 sets, or if we make your Lapel Pins, you can order as few as 24 sets.

We also offer Tie Tacks, Tie Clasps, Golf Ball Markers, Key Chains and more.
